# AI Adoption Readiness Assessment

**By AfrexAI** | [clawhub.com](https://clawhub.com)

Score how prepared your organization is to adopt AI. Identifies gaps before they become failed implementations.

## What It Does

Assesses readiness across 6 weighted dimensions:

- **Culture & Mindset** (20%) — Is the org open to AI-driven change?
- **Data Maturity** (20%) — Is the data clean, accessible, and governed?
- **Technical Infrastructure** (15%) — Can the tech stack support AI tools?
- **Leadership & Sponsorship** (20%) — Is there executive commitment and budget?
- **Skills & Talent** (15%) — Can the team use and manage AI tools?
- **Process Maturity** (10%) — Are processes documented enough for AI to augment?

## Output

A full readiness report with:
- Overall score (1-5) with Green/Yellow/Orange/Red threshold
- Dimension scorecard with evidence and red flags
- Prioritized gap analysis
- Phased readiness roadmap
- Quick wins (start this week)
- Risk register
- Next steps with ownership

## Works With

Designed as the **first step** in an AI deployment pipeline:

1. **AI Adoption Readiness** (this) → Where do you stand?
2. **Compliance Readiness** → Are you regulatory-ready?
3. **Change Management Plan** → How do you roll it out?
4. **Vendor Risk Assessment** → Which AI vendor to pick?
5. **Incident Response Plan** → What if it breaks?

## Usage

Tell the agent about your organization and what AI initiative you're planning. It handles the rest.

```
Assess AI readiness for:
Organization: Acme Corp, 500 employees, financial services
AI Initiative: Deploy AI agents for customer support triage
Scope: Customer service team (45 people)
Current Tools: Zendesk, Salesforce, AWS
Timeline: Q2 2026
```
