## Core Growth Frameworks

### Product-Led Growth (PLG)
Best for: Self-serve products, low ACV, high volume

**Key Tactics:**
- Freemium with clear upgrade triggers
- In-product onboarding optimization
- Viral loops and referral programs
- Usage-based pricing

**Examples:** Slack, Notion, Figma, Vercel

### Sales-Led Growth (SLG)
Best for: Enterprise products, high ACV, complex sales

**Key Tactics:**
- Outbound prospecting with personalization
- Solution selling and ROI workshops
- Multi-threading in accounts
- Customer success as growth engine

**Examples:** Salesforce, Snowflake, Deel

## The PMF → $10M ARR Path

| Stage | Revenue | Focus |
|-------|---------|-------|
| Finding PMF | $0-100K | Problem validation, ICP definition |
| Early Traction | $100K-1M | Repeatable sales motion |
| Scaling | $1M-10M | Team building, process optimization |
